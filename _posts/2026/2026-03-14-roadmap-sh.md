If you have ever felt overwhelmed trying to figure out what to learn next as a developer, <a href="https://roadmap.sh/">roadmap.sh</a> is the resource you have been looking for.

It is a community-driven platform that provides structured, visual roadmaps for nearly every role and skill in software engineering — from Frontend and Backend to DevOps, AI Engineering, Cybersecurity, and beyond.

<!--more-->

## What is roadmap.sh?

At its core, roadmap.sh offers **interactive roadmaps** that break down complex career paths into clear, step-by-step learning journeys. Each node on a roadmap links to curated resources so you always know *what* to learn and *where* to learn it.

Here is what the <a href="https://roadmap.sh/frontend">Frontend Developer roadmap</a> looks like:

<img src="/img/posts/frontend-developer-roadmap-sh.webp" alt="Frontend Developer roadmap on roadmap.sh" width="100%" />

The platform covers a wide range of topics:

- **Role-based roadmaps** — Frontend, Backend, Full Stack, DevOps, AI/ML Engineer, Data Engineer, Android, iOS, QA, and more.
- **Skill-based roadmaps** — React, Python, JavaScript, TypeScript, Docker, Kubernetes, SQL, System Design, and many others.
- **Guides** — Short, graphical explanations of specific engineering concepts like REST APIs, authentication patterns, or DNS.

Everything is open-source and constantly updated by the community, which means the content stays relevant as the industry evolves.

## Why I think it stands out

There are plenty of "learn to code" resources on the internet, but what makes roadmap.sh different is structure. Instead of handing you a random list of courses, it shows you the **big picture** first and then lets you zoom into each topic at your own pace.

Some features worth highlighting:

- **Progress tracking** — Sign in and mark topics as done, in progress, or skipped. Your roadmap turns into a personal checklist.
- **Custom roadmaps** — Use the built-in editor to draw your own roadmaps and share them with your team or community.
- **AI Tutor** — Get context-aware help while browsing any roadmap. Ask questions, get quiz summaries, and receive personalized course recommendations.
- **Projects & Best Practices** — Hands-on project ideas tied to each roadmap so you can apply what you learn.

## Who is it for?

Whether you are a **beginner** trying to break into tech, an **experienced developer** pivoting to a new specialty, or a **team lead** onboarding new engineers — roadmap.sh gives you a clear path forward.

It is completely free for the core roadmaps and guides. There is also a <a href="https://roadmap.sh/premium">Pro plan</a> that unlocks unlimited AI features, personalized coaching, and interview prep.

## Check it out

Head over to <a href="https://roadmap.sh/">roadmap.sh</a> and pick the roadmap that matches where you want to go. Bookmark it, track your progress, and stop wondering *"what should I learn next?"*
