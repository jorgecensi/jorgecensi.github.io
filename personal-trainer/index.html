    How it Works

    Tiers

    Core Fitness and Mat Pilates each have their own score from 0 to 90. Every 18 points unlocks the next tier, and the bars on the home screen show how far along you are.

    T1Beginner
    T2Novice
    T3Intermediate
    T4Advanced
    T5Elite

    Workouts draw exercises from your current tier and up to two tiers below it, so new challenges mix with familiar moves. Your Level stat is the average of both disciplines.

    Your feedback drives it

    After every workout you say how it felt, and both scores adjust:

    😴 Too easy+8 points
    💪 Just right+4 points
    🥵 Too hard−5 points

    Honest answers keep the plan at the right pace — sandbagging just makes next time too easy.

    What actually gets harder
    Tougher exercisesunlock at higher tiers
    Reps & hold timesgrow roughly every 15 points
    Rest between exercises20s → 15s (T2) → 12s (T4)

    Each exercise has its own cap, so doses grow to a sensible limit rather than forever.

    Streak

    Finish a workout within 48 hours of your last one and the streak keeps counting. Miss the window and it restarts at 1 — consistency beats intensity.

    Staying motivated
    🎯 Weekly goalpick 2–5 sessions, Mon–Sun
    ✨ Twistsevery workout gets a theme
    🏅 Achievementsbadges unlock as you train
    📊 Recordsbest hold & rep count per exercise
    🟩 The chainHistory marks every day you show up

    Focus days, endurance rounds, speedy rests and surprise finishers keep sessions fresh, while the weekly bar and the chain make consistency visible. A record is the biggest dose you actually finish — skipped exercises don't count — so your plank holds and rep counts climb as you do. Show up, tap done, keep the chain alive.
